About me

A Detroit native born with a passion for proving naysayers wrong. I moved 800 miles away to Boston, craving a challenge and with the hopes of beginning a career to help break the chains of poverty in my family. I began my Softeware Engineering career at Resilient Coders and joined Houghton Mifflin Harcourt as a Web Applications Developer shortly after. The determination to make a better life for my family, my competitive nature, and passion for learning has led me to sail on a journey to become a great Software Engineer.
